About Us!

Founded in 2021, we offer a variety of houseplants, seedlings, and gardening accessories made (or grown) by local vendors. Our mission is to provide the community with ethically sourced products that satisfy any plant lover's needs. Our storefront is located in downtown Wilmington, but we ship nationwide!
